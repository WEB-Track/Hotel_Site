The History Of Our Hotel

We opened in 2018 on November 14th. We were ready to service our new guests in our new hotel. Our hotel has 2 ballrooms and two 50,000 square foot conference rooms that can be breaking down into small rooms. There are two restaurants, one with a buffet style menu and other with full table service. We also have 500 rooms, 70 suites, 6 luxury suites with 180 degree views and 2 with 360 degree views. Amenities: two pools, wellness center and spa , hot tub, sauna, steam room and hotel shop.
